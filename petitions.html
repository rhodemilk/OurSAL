Petitions

Importance of Petitions

Petitioning is a way to push decision-makers in government to take action according to the petition. Also, the more people sign a petition, the more awareness reaches the public. This could eventually get public opinions or even political opinions to sway in support of causes brought to light with petitions. Websites like Change.org has petitions that eventually turned out successful with new laws created and passed in support of these victorious petitions. These websites also provide very simple ways to start and to sign petitions, with signatures done after basically one click.
