About Us

Tech Tigers

Tech Tigers is the name of our dynamic team in charge of OurSAL. Being locals in Florida, the environment is an important part of our lives. The Everglades was a model of diverse and thriving life here in South Florida, but upon more research, we discovered the harm of human's actions, as well as the harm of invasive species. This motivated us to use our skills in programming/computer science to inform and educate others on how important it is to take care of our local environment. Thank you for visiting our website!
